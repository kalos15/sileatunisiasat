Privacy Policy – ChessCamp

Last Updated: October 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how ChessCamp (“the App”), developed by Silea (“Developer”), manages user data. The App is designed for complete offline use and does not collect or share any personal information.

1. No Collection of Personal Information

The App operates entirely offline. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data such as names, emails, or locations. Internet permissions are not requested or used.

2. No Third-Party Services

  • No analytics or tracking tools are integrated.
  • No advertisements or third-party SDKs are included.
  • No social logins or data sharing features are present.

3. Local Data Storage

Minimal non-personal data such as game progress, board state, and user settings are stored locally on your device. This information remains private and is deleted when the App is uninstalled.

4. Children’s Privacy

ChessCamp is suitable for all ages and complies with children’s privacy standards by not collecting any personal data.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. The “Last Updated” date will always reflect the latest version.
